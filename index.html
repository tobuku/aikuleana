What is Kuleana?

Ai means "to eat" in Hawaiian. Kuleana means responsibility, care, and stewardship. Together, AiKuleana represents our commitment to caring about where we eat -- supporting local businesses, preserving Hawaiian culture, and encouraging everyone to experience authentic food across Oahu.

Every restaurant listed here is locally owned. Every story is real. Every recommendation comes from the community. When you eat local, you support families, neighborhoods, and traditions that make Hawaii special.
